About the Conference

International Conference on Sustainable Advanced Computing

The Department of Computer Science at
CHRIST (Deemed to be University) in Bangalore, India,
takes immense pride in organizing the biennial
"International Conference on Sustainable Advanced Computing"
in association with Springer CCIS
(Communications in Computer and Information Science) and
ACM (Association for Computing Machinery).

This conference serves as a melting pot for researchers, academics,
and industry professionals from around the world to
deliberate and collaborate on sustainable computing practices and innovations.

In an era where the world is facing pressing environmental concerns,
sustainable computing has emerged as a pivotal field,
addressing the environmental impact of modern technology.
The International Conference on Sustainable Advanced Computing
focuses on promoting eco-friendly and energy-efficient computing solutions
while advancing the state-of-the-art in various computing domains.

02-04, November’2023

IDSCS'23 is a technical gathering of the Data Science, Computer Science and Data security professionals to exchange the latest developments that are gaining momentum in the ever-growing global infrastructure of Computer Science. The conference is a multi-track program scheduled for two days. The conference will feature invited talks and selected paper presentations. IDSCS'23 aims to serve as a technical forum to share and learn about the various works that may lead to the Next generation IT and AI development. IDSCS'23 aims to serve as a technical forum to share and learn about the various works that may lead to the Next generation of Internet Security Infrastructure.

We invite original research submissions and findings based on large scale practical implementation on the following topics, but not limited to:

  • Sustainable AI and Machine Learning
  • Green Robotics
  • Sustainable Machine Learning for Sustainability
  • AI for Energy Management
  • AR and VR for Environmental Education
  • Virtual Labs and Simulations
  • Sustainable AI in Healthcare
  • Robotics in Sustainable Agriculture
  • Ethics and Responsibility in AI
  • Digital Twins and Sustainability
  • Sustainable AI Hardware
  • Sustainable AI Policies and Regulations
  • Smart Cities and Sustainable Technologies
  • AI-Driven Environmental Monitoring
  • Eco-Friendly Robotics in Education
