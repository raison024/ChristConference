About the Conference

International Conference on Sustainable Advanced Computing

In the ever-evolving landscape of advanced computing, sustainability has emerged as a defining imperative. The International Conference on Sustainable Advanced Computing (ICSAC) is conceived as a beacon in this domain, dedicated to advancing the cause of sustainable computing. ICSAC aims to provide a multidisciplinary platform where researchers, practitioners, policymakers, and industry leaders converge to explore, discuss, and innovate sustainable solutions within the realm of advanced computing. ICSAC recognizes that sustainability is no longer an option but an imperative for the future of computing. Sustainability encompasses ecological responsibility, ethical computing practices, and social inclusivity. The conference's theme, "Advancing Sustainability in Advanced Computing: Innovation, Ethics, and Inclusion," reflects this holistic approach, emphasizing the need for innovative solutions, ethical frameworks, and equitable access.

22-23, March’2024

This conference is conducted by CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bangalore, India. It would provide an opportunity to spotlight research ideas to an international community as well as be a platform to get abreast on the latest trends in the field of Business and Computing Sciences. In the last three decades, numerous technologies have surfaced replacing the older ones and also getting themselves replaced rapidly, while on the other hand, alternate technologies with competitive affordability also have been produced swiftly. Therefore, the challenge for new technology is its sustainability and affordability. This conference aims in exploring research outputs that are bringing out new technologies that are both sustainable and affordable to the millions who consume them or make a commodity of them. It aims to offer an international platform for academicians, researchers, engineers, entrepreneurs, industry experts, and budding research scholars around the world to share their research findings with global experts. Such a gathering will also help the delegates to establish research or business relations as well as to find international linkage for future collaborations in their career path. This Conference will be carried out in a hybrid format, allowing world-scattered researchers, academicians, business and industry professionals, and policymakers to participate in this Conference.

We invite original research submissions and findings based on large scale practical implementation on the following topics, but not limited to:

  • Sustainable AI and Machine Learning
  • Green Robotics
  • Sustainable Machine Learning for Sustainability
  • AI for Energy Management
  • AR and VR for Environmental Education
  • Virtual Labs and Simulations
  • Sustainable AI in Healthcare
  • Robotics in Sustainable Agriculture
  • Ethics and Responsibility in AI
  • Digital Twins and Sustainability
  • Sustainable AI Hardware
  • Sustainable AI Policies and Regulations
  • Smart Cities and Sustainable Technologies
  • AI-Driven Environmental Monitoring
  • Eco-Friendly Robotics in Education
